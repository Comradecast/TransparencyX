# Data Sources

## Primary Official Sources
- **House Clerk Financial Disclosure Reports:** Official repository for the U.S. House of Representatives.
- **Senate eFD / Senate public disclosure search:** Official electronic repository for the U.S. Senate.

## Legal Context
- **EIGA (Ethics in Government Act of 1978):** Establishes the framework for financial disclosures for federal officials.
- **STOCK Act (Stop Trading on Congressional Knowledge Act of 2012):** Requires more frequent reporting of certain financial transactions.

## Known Limitations
Official data is public but highly fragmented across different formats (XML, PDF, scanned images) and inconsistent between the House and Senate.
